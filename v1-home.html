06 · Our Story

From East Africa
to a continental movement.

Founded in 2010 to accelerate trade in the East African Community, TMA now spans 17 countries — and our 2023–2030 strategy is just getting started.

2010
TMA 1.0 · Foundation
TradeMark East Africa established as the aid-for-trade vehicle for the East African Community.
2018
TMA 2.0 · Expansion
Operations expand across the Horn of Africa. First one-stop border posts go live; Mombasa dwell time begins to drop.
2023
TMA 3.0 · Continental
Rebrand to TradeMark Africa, West Africa launch in Ghana, Trade Catalyst Africa stood up as catalytic finance arm.
2030
TMA 4.0 · The horizon
Strategy 2023–2030: $1.3B mobilised, 17 countries active, low-carbon corridors live, and the AfCFTA fully operational.
